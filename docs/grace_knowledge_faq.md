# BestBottles FAQ & Company Policies
(Source of Truth for Grace AI Agent & Customer Support)

## Ordering & Minimums
* **Ordering Method:** Online ordering is preferred for speed and accuracy. Phone orders can result in errors.
* **Minimum Order:** There is a **$50.00 minimum order requirement** (excluding shipping charges).
* **Sample Orders:** Customers with verified businesses can purchase samples below the minimum. They must email `sales@nematinternational.com` with item codes. Invoices are paid via PayPal or by calling in a credit card.
* **Out of Stock Items:** ETAs are provided on the product page when available. Most items remain in stock, but some may be temporarily out or discontinued by the manufacturer.

## Shipping & Fulfillment
* **Warehouse Pickup:** Yes, in Union City, CA. Customers must call at least 1 day ahead. Pickup hours: **10:30am to 3:00pm**.
* **Carriers & Methods:** Mostly **UPS** (Ground, 3-Day, 2-Day, Overnight) or **USPS**. *Note: UPS does not deliver to P.O. Boxes or APO/AFO addresses.*
* **Box Weight Limit:** In general, packages are not packed heavier than **40 lbs** each.
* **Processing Time:** Domestic orders take **2-3 business days** to process, and **1-5 business days** to ship based on location.
* **Peak Season (Sept - Dec):** Processing can take up to **4-5 business days** before shipping.
* **Same Day Shipping:** Available for a mandatory **$15 fee**. Must order before **11:00am PST**. Must be requested by calling 800-936-3628 (not accepted via email). 
  * *Not available for:* International orders, personalized products, weekends/holidays, large/oversize/special order items.
* **Address Errors/Refusals:** Refused deliveries or incorrect addresses incur a **15% restocking fee**. Shipping charges are non-refundable.
* **Signature Confirmation:** Optional ($3.00 fee). Highly recommended for apartments/condos. If declined, the customer assumes full liability for lost/stolen packages marked as delivered.

## Returns & Claims
* **Return Window:** Must be returned within **15 days** of receipt.
* **Restocking Fee:** **15% restocking fee** applies to returns. Shipping/handling charges are non-refundable. Customer pays return shipping. 
* **Items NOT Eligible For Return:** Personalized products, used items, or items damaged during return shipping. Returns policy does not apply to international orders.
* **Damaged/Defective Claims:** Must be filed within **7 business days** of receipt via email (with pictures) or phone. Nemat International is not responsible for carrier damage but will assist with claims.
* **Missing Items:** Glass bottles and components (bottle, plug, cap) are often shipped separately or in multiple boxes. Must notify within **48 hours** if items are missing after a thorough check. Investigation takes 6-8 business days.
* **Order Changes/Cancellations:** Email `sales@nematinternational.com` ASAP with order number. Once shipped, changes cannot be accommodated.

## International Orders
* **Carriers:** UPS International Economy, UPS Priority International, or US Priority Mail (for Russia and certain other countries).
* **Duties/Taxes/Brokerage:** The customer is fully responsible for all duties, taxes, and UPS brokerage fees upon delivery. 
* **Canada Shipping:** UPS International Economy/Priority is preferred. UPS Ground to Canada often ends up being more expensive because of additional UPS brokerage fees charged to the customer upon delivery.

## Payments & Taxes
* **Methods Accepted:** PayPal, Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Discover, Business Checks (orders wait to ship until check clears). Bank Wire Transfer used for large/international orders. Prepaid/Gift cards not accepted unless registered with a billing address.
* **Currency:** All prices are in US Dollars.
* **Sales Tax:** Charged only for orders shipped within California. CA Resellers must provide a valid CA resale license number via email or fax.
